Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SearchWars ("the App") is a trivia game developed by Shaz Moghaddam that asks players to guess which of two items receives more Google searches. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to it.

Information we collect

SearchWars is designed with privacy in mind. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The only data stored is:

  • High scores — saved locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
  • PWA install preference — a single flag stored in your browser's local storage to avoid showing the "Add to Home Screen" hint repeatedly.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

Third-party services

The App does not currently integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. If this changes in a future update (for example, the addition of optional rewarded ads), this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an in-app notice.

Children's privacy

SearchWars is rated Everyone (E) and is suitable for all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it complies with children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-K.

Data security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and no personal information is transmitted to our servers, the risk of a data breach is minimal. Your high scores are stored using your operating system's standard local storage mechanism.

Your rights

As we do not collect or store any personal data on our end, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on your behalf. To clear your local high scores, you can clear the App's local storage via your device's app settings, or uninstall and reinstall the App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if new features are added (such as leaderboards, accounts, or advertising). We will indicate the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising from the use of SearchWars shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
