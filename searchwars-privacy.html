SearchWars – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

SearchWars ("the App") is developed and maintained by Shaz Moghaddam. This privacy policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information when you submit a score to the leaderboard:

  • Player name — the name you choose to enter voluntarily
  • Score — your game score
  • Category — the category you played
  • Timestamp — the date and time the score was submitted

No account registration is required. No email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to display the global leaderboard within the App. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage

Leaderboard data is stored securely using Google Firebase Realtime Database, hosted in the European Union (Belgium). Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Leaderboard entries (player name and score) are visible to all users of the App as part of the public leaderboard feature.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The leaderboard name entry is voluntary and players are advised not to use their real name.

6. Advertising

The App does not display any advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs.

7. Your Rights

If you would like your leaderboard entry removed, please contact us at shaz.moghaddam@gmail.com with the name and score you submitted and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
